Health & Wellness

Improving your nutrition with Mulgowie fresh quality produce.

As a part of our commitment to improving the nutrition of all Australians, Mulgowie gives you and your family not only what is needed, but also what is good for you and those you love.

A healthy lifestyle begins with you,
it's as easy as 1,2,3

You have what it takes to maintain a healthy lifestyle because maintaining a healthy lifestyle starts with you. It’s about making good choices for you and your family in what, as well as how much, you eat. 

1. Eat MORE of these

Your health will benefit if you eat more fruit and vegetables and more natural foods derived from plant sources.

Think of including vegetables in all meal occasions, breakfast, lunch and dinner.    They also make great in-between meal snacks.  

To ensure you receive an abundance of nutrients, eat a variety of fruits and vegetables including all colours of the rainbow.

2. Eat LESS of these 

Decrease your intake of fast foods and processed food.

These foods typically contain preservatives, have high sodium levels and are high in saturated fats and sugar.

3.  Look after yourself 

Maintain an active lifestyle, drink plenty of water and sleep at least eight hours a night - these are all critical to maintaining good health and a positive outlook.

If you need further information, an Accredited Practising Dietician can provide expert nutrition and dietary advice.
